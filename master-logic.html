Pattern guide

Compare the direction of each argument: from a group to a case, from cases to a group, or between similar cases.

Statistical syllogismMost F are G; x is F; therefore, x is probably G.
Inductive generalizationMost examined F are G in a representative sample; therefore, most F are G.
Combined generalizationA generalization from a sample is applied to a particular case.
Analogical inferencex and y are similar; a relevant truth about x is projected onto y.
